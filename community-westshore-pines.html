About the community

Room to build, room to breathe

Westshore Pines Ranches offers spacious homesites and larger acreage tracts surrounded by mature Florida pine, with lakefront and water-view parcels available. It's an ideal setting for a primary home, a weekend retreat, or a long-term hold in one of the country's fastest-growing states.

Every purchase is handled with the same transparency and care that defines Westshore Land Sales — clear information, friendly guidance, and no pressure from your first question through closing.

  • Large wooded and lakeside homesites
  • Professional property surveys
  • Title work and warranty deeds (where applicable)
  • Financing assistance available
  • Local, state, and federal approvals when required
