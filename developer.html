About Westshore Land Sales

Who we are

Founded in 2014, Westshore Land Sales has spent over a decade acquiring and developing land across Florida — creating opportunities for homesites, ranch land, small farms, hunting and outdoor recreation, and wildlife conservation.

Every property we offer is carefully selected with value, usability, and future potential in mind. Our goal is to provide buyers with confidence and peace of mind throughout the entire process, from your first inquiry through closing.
